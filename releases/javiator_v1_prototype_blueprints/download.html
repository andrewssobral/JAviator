JAviator V1 Prototype Blueprints

The following thumbnails are previews of existing blueprints for the JAviator V1 quadrotor, which was designed to serve as proof of concept. The original blueprints for this prototype version can be downloaded in three different file formats: CorelDRAW (CDR), Encapsulated PostScript (EPS), and Portable Document Format (PDF).
