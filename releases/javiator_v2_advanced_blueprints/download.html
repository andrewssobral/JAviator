JAviator V2 Advanced Blueprints

The following thumbnails are previews of existing blueprints for the JAviator V2 quadrotor, which was designed for the series production. The original blueprints for this advanced version can be downloaded in three different file formats: CorelDRAW (CDR), Encapsulated PostScript (EPS), and Portable Document Format (PDF).
